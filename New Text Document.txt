# THE ACADEMUS STRUGGLE — 6 YEARS

Shumyle joined Academus International School as IT Administrator in November 2020. The school had 20 students when he started. Over 6 years, he single-handedly built it to 3 branches, 850+ students, 80 staff, 40 domestic workers.

## What He Built Alone — Unsupported, Unrecognised

- Built entire IT infrastructure across multiple floors

- Managed 126 computers, networks, Google Workspace for 470+ students and 80 staff

- Ran school cafe voluntarily from August 2025 — without pay, authority, or recognition

- Generated Google Meet links for 40 sections over Eid holiday — on personal day off

- Reset 470+ student passwords during Eid break — on his own time

- Handled 215+ IT queries in a single day during hybrid transition

- Built full hybrid learning infrastructure during online classes

## The Narcissistic Principal — Sarah

- Manufactured crises to keep Shumyle reactive and dependent

- Denied preparation time then blamed him for failures

- Used flying monkeys (floor managers) to monitor and report

- Attacked him the day after he shared personal happiness — consistent pattern

- Attempted to have him train his replacement without his knowledge

- Called him 'redundant' — said report card is the only thing keeping him relevant

- Gave him responsibility without authority — domestic staff told not to help him

- Reduced his tea from 2 cups to 1 — offered her own tea bags as 'charity'

## Trauma Responses Identified Through Coaching

- Fawning — self-blame and people-pleasing

- Hypervigilance expressed through productivity — working to feel safe

- Dysregulation — body shaking, clenching, numbness when triggered

# THE RESIGNATION — April 1, 2026

## The Final Straw — March 31, 2026

Sarah called Shumyle in, unusually nice, asked him to lead the new session's report card project and come the next morning with Soban (his replacement) to plan. Same day, accountant Fahad secretly assigned an office boy to conduct IT inventory without telling Shumyle — then denied it to his face. Sarah protected Fahad and interrogated Shumyle instead.

**Shumyle****'****s words: **'I realized yeah I have been a fool. I understand people's feelings but I'm unable to understand my own feelings. I started feeling sick, heavy, cloudy head, stress, anger, all emotions. I knew tomorrow I will be training my own replacement.'

## The Decision

He went home, broke down. Wife Natasha hugged him. Mother cried. Brother Minhal — home by fate — supported him. All three told him to leave. He resigned that night — April 1, 2026 — via email to all directors, principal, and floor managers.

## The Night

**Shumyle****'****s words: **'The night I resigned, I went home like the Hulk. I froze on my bed. My body was shaking. My mother cried when she saw only 10% of what I was carrying. I'm glad she didn't see the other 90% — she could have fainted.'

# NAVIGATING THE NOTICE PERIOD — Frameworks

- **Grey Rock: **Become uninteresting. No emotion. Short, flat, neutral answers.

- **Broken Record: **'My decision is final and I am committed to a smooth handover.'

- **Director Shield: **'I would prefer to discuss this in the presence of the directors.'

- **Physical Reset: **Feet flat on floor. 4-4-4 breathing. Fixed point.

## April 4 — The Water Break (Defining Moment)

Sarah called him in. Three cafe staff waiting outside. She started shouting — red eyes, waving hands. Shumyle said: 'Wait wait wait. Let me drink water.' Walked out mid-shouting. Greeted people in the corridor. Came back with half a glass. Called the director's PA on loudspeaker. Gave clean factual account. When she called cafe staff back without him — he walked out with them. Wrote in his diary and scrolled phone while she talked. Completely unbothered.

## The Camera Trap He Avoided

After Jumma prayer, heard Sarah rushing out with a group. Instantly understood — she wanted to create a narrative that he was alone in the building. He ran downstairs, marked attendance exit on camera, and walked out with the group. 'I got recorded on camera that I left with them so they don't get to create any story.'
